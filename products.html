Bovine Raw Material



Some examples of bovine raw material we can supply and their potential medical and pharmaceutical applications include:

• corium (from hide) for making Type 1 and Type 3 collagen products
• tendon for making Type 1 collagen products
• heart for making heart valves
• pericardium for making heart valves, vascular patches, bone and dental membranes and surgical buttressing
• intestines for making sutures

As certain properties of bovine raw material may vary with age and this variation may be relevant for its intended application, we can supply raw material sourced from bovine animals of any age up to 30 months old.
