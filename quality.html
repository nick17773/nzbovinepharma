Quality Management



All our ASM and XM products are:

- US FDA-registered with Document Master File (“DMF”) number
- US FDA closed herd-compliant “Source Animal, Product, Preclinical, and Clinical Issues Concerning the Use of Transplantation Products in Humans” Guidance for Industry for enhanced traceability, December 2016
- Medical / pharmaceutical grade
- Exclusively sourced from bovine animals that have been bred, raised, harvested and processed in New Zealand


We hold the following US FDA-registered DMF number:

Center: Center for Devices and Radiological Health
DMF Number: MAF3055
Issued: 29 October 2018
Device: Type V (ASM and XM)

We have successfully met the US FDA recommendations in regards to the pedigree and handling procedures of ASM and XM used in medical device submissions.


Each farm operates under Standard Operating Procedures (cGMP) which ensure:

- source animals are not exposed to potential carriers of diseases or parasites from outside the herd
- no growth stimulating hormones or GMO
- no unnecessary insecticides, pesticides or antibiotics
- each source animal is listed and documented
- each source animal is checked and monitored daily for any signs of illness


Quality systems are registered with the US FDA (with ISO 22442 pending). All ASM and XM extracted can be traced to:

- a specific source animal
- pedigree for 2 generations (parents and grandparents)
- health
- food and water consumption
- much more


Complete traceability - Each source animal is tagged with a radio frequency identification (“RFID”) tag containing a microchip with a 15-digit RFID number that is globally unique and can be read by an RFID scanner.

Each harvesting and processing facility operates under Standard Operating Procedures (cGMP) which include:

- full inspection and veterinary exam, before harvest
- NZ MPI inspection of each source animal’s organs and flesh, plus a full necropsy by an onsite veterinarian
- ASM and XM are removed from the harvesting area for processing / packaging

Further processing of ASM into more refined and finished pharmaceuticals, devices or biologics may occur offsite.
