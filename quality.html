Bovine Collagen Products



Some examples of bovine collagen products we can procure the supply of and their potential medical and pharmaceutical applications include:

• Type 1 atelo collagen (solution and lyophilized) for injectibles, dermal fillers and 3D printing
• collagen pads for skin and tissue regeneration
• collagen coatings and impregnation of medical devices for dental and orthopedic surgery
• purified collagen solutions, dispersions and dough/paste for orthopedic surgery
• collagen fibre and milled collagen for oral surgery

We can also procure the transfer of technology for making such products. This may be of interest for customers with their own manufacturing capability.
